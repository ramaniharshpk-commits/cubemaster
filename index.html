Different Types of Cubes

Learn about the most popular twisty puzzles.

3×3 Cube

The classic Rubik's Cube. 3 layers per side and the most widely recognized puzzle.

2×2 Cube

Also called the Pocket Cube. Smaller and easier than the 3×3, great for beginners.

4×4 Cube

Known as the Rubik's Revenge. More complex with 4 layers per side, challenging even for experienced solvers.

Pyraminx

A pyramid-shaped puzzle. Twists along its tips and edges, offering a unique solving experience.

Megaminx

A dodecahedron-shaped puzzle with 12 sides. More pieces, more complexity, and more fun!
